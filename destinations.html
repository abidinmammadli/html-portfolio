Popular destinations

Turkey

  • Istanbul: A vibrant city straddling Europe and Asia, famous for its rich history, stunning mosques, and bustling bazaars.

  • Cappadocia: Known for its unique rock formations, hot air balloon rides, and fascinating cave dwellings.

  • Pamukkale: Famous for its thermal mineral waters and stunning white travertine terraces.

United Arab Emirates

  • Dubai: Enjoy luxury shopping, modern architecture, and thrilling attractions like the Burj Khalifa and desert safaris.

  • Abu Dhabi: Visit the stunning Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque, the Louvre Abu Dhabi, and enjoy beautiful beaches.


Russia

  • Moscow: Explore the historic Red Square, the Kremlin, and iconic landmarks like St. Basil's Cathedral.

  • * St. Petersburg: Visit the Hermitage Museum, take a canal tour, and admire the beautiful architecture of this cultural capital.
